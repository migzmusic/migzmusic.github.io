Strummin' 2023

My Role

In the charity concert, I served as the guitar instructor. During the initial rehearsals, I guided guitarists in mastering chords and strumming patterns. In the later rehearsals, I focused on refining songs for better synchronization with singers and the band. These efforts contributed to a polished and cohesive performance, highlighting my commitment to music in philanthropic endeavors.

Cover Songs

My Process

When covering songs, I prioritize capturing genuine emotion over complexity. For Coldplay's "Yellow," I go for a slower, bassier vibe, while Elton John's "I'm Still Standing" gets a lively touch with higher notes and a bit of complexity. It's about evoking the right feelings and enhancing the musical experience based on each song's unique character.
